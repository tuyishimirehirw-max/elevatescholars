About Me

My journey with The Voices Of Future Rwanda began with a passion for expressing ideas and a desire to make an impact. Through the University & Professional program, I've learned that leadership is not just about speaking—it's about listening, understanding, and inspiring action.

From participating in advanced debate competitions to completing leadership certifications, I've developed skills in critical thinking, persuasive communication, and ethical leadership. These experiences have shaped me into a confident speaker and a thoughtful leader who believes in the power of youth voices to drive change.

I am currently preparing for university applications with aspirations to study international relations and leadership development. The Voices Of Future Rwanda has equipped me with the tools to articulate my vision and the courage to pursue it.
