Which Program is Right for You?

Find your perfect fit based on your goals and experience level

Choose Junior Orators if...

  • Your child is in primary school (ages 7-12)
  • You want to build foundational confidence
  • They're shy or hesitant to speak up
  • You prefer a playful, interactive approach

Choose Global Scholars if...

  • Your student is in high school (ages 13-18)
  • You're preparing for university applications
  • You want competitive debate training
  • Building a portfolio is important

Choose Executive Communicator if...

  • You're in university or working professionally
  • You need executive presence skills
  • You pitch to investors or lead meetings
  • Professional certification matters
