Lesson 9.4: Crafting Your Opening Statement

Learn how to create powerful opening statements that capture attention and set the tone for your presentation.

Key Takeaways

1

Hook Your Audience

Start with a compelling question, statistic, or story that immediately grabs attention and relates to your topic.

2

State Your Purpose Clearly

Within the first 30 seconds, your audience should know exactly what you'll be discussing and why it matters.

3

Preview Your Main Points

Give your audience a roadmap of what's to come. This helps them follow your presentation and stay engaged.

4

Connect with Agaciro

Incorporate values of dignity and self-worth into your opening to establish credibility and cultural relevance.

Practice Exercise

Write a 60-second opening statement for a presentation on "The Importance of Youth Leadership in Rwanda." Use the techniques you learned in this lesson.
