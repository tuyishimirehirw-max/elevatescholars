Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How do I enroll my child or myself?

Click the "Enroll Now" button on any page to start our multi-step registration process. You'll select your program, provide student information, and choose a payment method. For schools interested in partnerships, please use the contact form to book a demo.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Bank Transfer, and card payments online. Monthly and term payment plans are available.

How long does each program last?

Junior Orators: 12 weeks | High School: 16 weeks | University & Professional: 8 weeks intensive. All programs include certification upon completion.

Are the certifications recognized?

Yes! Our certifications are valuable for university applications and professional portfolios. We also provide digital portfolios that students can share with universities and employers.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full refund if you withdraw within the first 2 weeks of the program. After that, refunds are prorated based on sessions attended. Contact us for specific cases.

Can schools partner with The Voices Of Future Rwanda?

Absolutely! We offer turn-key partnerships where we provide trainers, curriculum, and competition management. Schools provide the venue and students. Contact us to book a demo session.
