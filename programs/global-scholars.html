16-Week Curriculum Breakdown

A comprehensive journey from fundamentals to tournament competition

Weeks 1-4: Communication Foundations

Week 1: Orientation & Confidence

Program introduction, ice-breakers, public speaking basics, overcoming stage fright

Week 2: Speech Structure

Introduction-body-conclusion, thesis statements, supporting points, transitions

Week 3: Voice & Body Language

Vocal variety, pace, tone, gestures, eye contact, stage presence

Week 4: Persuasive Speaking

Ethos, pathos, logos, persuasion techniques, audience analysis

Weeks 5-8: Critical Thinking & Logic

Week 5: Critical Thinking Fundamentals

Facts vs opinions, assumptions, inferences, analyzing arguments

Week 6: Logic & Fallacies

Logical reasoning, common fallacies (ad hominem, straw man, slippery slope), sound arguments

Week 7: Leadership Styles

Different leadership approaches, ethical leadership, leading by example

Week 8: Team Leadership

Conflict management, team dynamics, decision-making, collaboration

Weeks 9-12: Debate Mastery

Week 9: Debate Basics (WSDC Introduction)

World Schools format, roles, timing, POIs, debate etiquette

Week 10: Building Arguments

Constructing strong arguments, claim-warrant-impact, case building

Week 11: Rebuttal Skills

Attacking opponent arguments, clash, extensions, defensive rebuttal

Week 12: Evidence & Research

Finding credible sources, citing evidence, research methodology

Weeks 13-16: Competition & Certification

Week 13: Practice Debates

Full-format practice rounds, peer feedback, judge feedback

Week 14: Civic & Social Issues

Understanding current events, social justice, global citizenship

Week 15: Elevate Cup Preparation

Tournament prep, strategy sessions, final skill refinement

Week 16: Elevate Cup & Certification

Mock tournament competition, recorded feedback, certificate ceremony

Final Competition

The Elevate Cup Tournament

Every Global Scholars cohort culminates in the Elevate Cup—our prestigious mock tournament where students compete in front of judges, receive detailed feedback, and have their performances recorded for university portfolios.

Winners earn special recognition, but every participant walks away with professional feedback and video recordings perfect for university applications.
