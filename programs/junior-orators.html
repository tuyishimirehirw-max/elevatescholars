Learning through games, role-play, and fun activities that keep children engaged and excited to participate.

Content designed specifically for 7-12 year olds, using language and concepts that match their developmental stage.

Confidence-First

12-Week Curriculum Breakdown

A structured journey from first words to confident expression

Weeks 1-3: Finding Your Voice

Week 1: Welcome & Confidence Building

Introduction to the program, ice-breaker games, learning each other's names, creating a safe space.

Week 2: Speaking Clearly (Voice and Body)

Vocal projection exercises, posture practice, eye contact games, expressing emotions through voice.

Week 3: Listening Skills (Listening with Respect)

Active listening activities, turn-taking games, asking questions politely, showing respect when others speak.

Weeks 4-6: Storytelling & Expression

Week 4: Storytelling Basics

Beginning, middle, end structure; describing people and places; using descriptive language.

Week 5: Expressing Feelings & Ideas

Talking about emotions, sharing opinions, explaining why you think something, expressing preferences.

Week 6: Introduction to Leadership

Understanding leadership as service, taking responsibility, teamwork basics, helping others.

Weeks 7-9: Teamwork & Debate Basics

Week 7: Teamwork and Cooperation

Working in groups, sharing roles, collaborative problem-solving, supporting teammates.

Week 8: Giving Opinions and Reasons

"I think... because..." structure, backing up ideas with simple reasons, respectful disagreement.

Week 9: Introduction to Debating

Friendly debates on fun topics, agreeing and disagreeing politely, taking turns to speak.

Weeks 10-12: Showcase & Celebration

Week 10: Public Speaking Practice

Preparing "My First Speech," practicing in front of the class, giving and receiving encouragement.

Week 11: Showcase Preparation

Rehearsing for Elevate Day, finalizing speeches, preparing for parent showcase event.

Week 12: Showcase & Celebration (Elevate Day)

Final presentations in front of family, certificate ceremony, celebration of growth and achievement!

Sample Fun-Debate Topics

Age-appropriate motions that spark curiosity and critical thinking

"Homework is good for children"

"Children should have pets"

"School uniforms are important"

Note: All debate topics are carefully selected to be age-appropriate, engaging, and relevant to children's daily lives.
