Our Story

From First Words to Future Leaders

The journey of a movement dedicated to transforming Rwanda's youth through the power of Agaciro—dignity, self-worth, and the courage to lead.

The Spark

Where It All Began

The Voices Of Future Rwanda was born from a simple but powerful realization: The future of Rwanda belongs to those who can articulate it.

Our founder's journey began in the heart of the Rwandan debate movement—at I Debate Rwanda. It was there that we witnessed the transformative power of a single voice. We saw shy students become champions, and hesitant thinkers become decisive leaders.

We learned that debate is not just about winning arguments; it is about the "Agaciro" (dignity) of self-expression and the courage to disagree with grace.

The Evolution

Adapting to a Changing World

As the world changed, we saw a growing gap. While our students were brilliant, they needed more than just debate skills to succeed in a globalized, digital economy. They required professional poise, executive leadership, and the ability to pitch ideas to investors and world leaders.

We realized that for Rwanda to reach its Vision 2050 goals, our scholars needed an academy that blended the rigor of competitive debate with the practical demands of modern leadership.

"Vision 2050 isn't just a government policy—it's a call to action for every Rwandan to contribute to national transformation."

Why "Elevate"?

Our name reflects our sole mission: to lift every student to their highest potential

Primary Students

We elevate Primary students by giving them their first taste of confidence—teaching them that their voice matters and their ideas have value.

High Scholars

We elevate High Scholars by preparing them for the world's most prestigious universities—giving them the academic gravitas to compete globally.

University Students

We elevate University Students by turning them into the executives and entrepreneurs of tomorrow—ready to lead Rwanda's next chapter.

Our Commitment

More Than a Training Center

Today, The Voices Of Future Rwanda stands as a premier academy where logic meets leadership. We are more than a training center; we are a community of thinkers, dreamers, and doers.

We believe that when you give a young Rwandan a microphone and a structured mind, you aren't just training a student—you are empowering a nation.

Meet Our Founder

The vision behind The Voices Of Future Rwanda

NTWARI Derrick

Founder & Lead Coach

NTWARI Derrick's journey into debate began at College Immaculée Conception/Save, where he discovered the transformative power of structured argumentation and public speaking. His passion ignited through competitive debate led him to I Debate Rwanda, where he witnessed firsthand how a single confident voice could change a student's entire trajectory.

Recognizing the gap between traditional debate skills and the demands of modern leadership, Derrick founded The Voices Of Future Rwanda to create a comprehensive academy that develops not just debaters, but confident communicators ready to lead in any context—from the classroom to the boardroom.

His vision: Every young Rwandan deserves the tools to articulate their dreams and lead with dignity.
