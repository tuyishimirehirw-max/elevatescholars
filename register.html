Terms and Conditions

By enrolling in The Voices Of Future Rwanda, you agree to the following:

1. Program Commitment: Students are expected to attend all scheduled sessions and complete assigned work.

2. Payment Terms: Program fees are due as outlined in your enrollment confirmation. Refunds are available within the first 2 weeks of the program.

3. Code of Conduct: All scholars must maintain respectful behavior and uphold the values of Agaciro (dignity).

4. Photography & Media: We may photograph or record sessions for promotional and educational purposes. Please inform us if you do not consent.

5. Data Privacy: Your information will be used solely for program administration and will not be shared with third parties without consent.
